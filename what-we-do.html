Ghana's forests are disappearing at an alarming rate. We're on the front lines, working with communities to protect and restore vital ecosystems that sustain life, combat climate change, and provide livelihoods.

Our environmental programs include:

  • Tree planting and reforestation campaigns across degraded lands
  • Community-based forest protection initiatives
  • Environmental education in schools and communities
  • Watershed protection and water resource management
  • Wildlife conservation awareness programs
  • Advocacy for environmental policies and regulations

Thematic Area 02

üåæ Sustainable Agriculture

Agriculture is the backbone of Ghana's economy, but unsustainable practices threaten both the environment and farmers' livelihoods. We empower smallholder farmers with knowledge and tools for sustainable, climate-smart farming.

Our agricultural programs include:

  • Training in organic and sustainable farming techniques
  • Soil conservation and fertility management education
  • Agroforestry and tree-crop integration systems
  • Water-efficient irrigation methods and rainwater harvesting
  • Sustainable livestock management practices
  • Linking farmers to markets for fair prices

  • Maternal and child health support programs
