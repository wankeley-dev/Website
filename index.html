About SOTE

Empowering Communities Through Environmental Action

Founded in 2005, Soldiers of the Environment (SOTE) is a dedicated non-governmental organization based in Ghana. Our mission is to protect and restore our natural environment while empowering local communities with sustainable practices.

We believe that environmental conservation goes hand-in-hand with community development. Through education, reforestation, and health initiatives, we're building a greener, healthier future for all Ghanaians.

  • Environmental conservation & reforestation
  • Sustainable agriculture training
  • Community health education programs
  • Youth empowerment initiatives
